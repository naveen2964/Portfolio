Movie Recommendation System

This project is a Movie Recommender System built with Python and Streamlit. It uses BERT embeddings and cosine similarity to recommend movies that are similar to a selected movie. Recommender systems are tools used in applications to suggest products, movies, music, and more to users based on their past behavior or behavior of similar users. They play a crucial role in the field of personalized content delivery and are widely used in various domains such as e-commerce, entertainment, and social media platforms.
